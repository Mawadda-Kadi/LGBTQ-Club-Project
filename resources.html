LGBTQIA+|Handbook Germany:

This brilliant official website provides you with information on how to protect your rights in Germany as a queer individual.

It also gives you information about what to do against discrimination, what transgender individuals are entitled to, where to seek support, and much more.

Lesben- und Schwulenverband in Deutschland (LSVD):

LSVD is a nationwide organization that advocates for the rights of lesbians, gays, and bisexual and transgender people.

It is based in Köln.

Read more..

Lesbenberatung Berlin e.V.:

It is an orginisation that offers counselling for lesbians, bisexual women, trans*, inter*, non-binary and queer women.

Their website supports more than 10 languages.

It based in Berlin.

FluchtGrund Queer:

A great informative website for Queer Refugees for LGBTIQA+ Youth.

You can find Materials for refugees, institutions and supporters, information about LGBTI people in the world, and materials for language learners and integration courses

Read more..

Trans inter Queer:

TrIQ is a social center and self-advocacy organization by and for trans*, inter* and non-binary people.

It is based in Berlin.

Read more..

Fliederlich - Verein für lesbische, schwule, bisexuelle, trans, inter und queere Menschen mit Kindern

Fliederlich is an organization that focuses on the intersection of LGBT identities and parenting.

They provide support for LGBT families, including counselling and educational resources.

It is based in Nürnberg.
