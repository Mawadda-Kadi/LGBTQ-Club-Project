DramaQueers

DramaQueers are a monthly meeting for LGBTQ+ theatre makers, actors, directors and writers.

The group runs workshops and drama practice for people of all levels once a month at the Centre.

Youth Club

A regular group for LGBTQ+ people aged 13-19.

We can help to understand your identity and share experiences of coming out.

We discuss topics including bullying, feeling safe, life at home, education and work.

Pride Parade

CSD Deutschland (Christopher Street Day) is an annual event celebrated in various German cities to advocate for the rights of LGBTQ+ people.

We gather at the LGPTQ+ Club at 10:00, then we march together.
