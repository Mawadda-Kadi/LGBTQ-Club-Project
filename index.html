Welcome to LGBTQ+ Club

We are thrilled to have you here. Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive space that celebrates the diversity of the LGBTQ community. Whether you're here to find resources, connect with others, or stay updated on events, you are an essential part of our vibrant community. Explore our site, engage with the content, and join us in our commitment to fostering understanding, acceptance, and equality for all. Together, we can make a difference!

Our Vision

A more connected, belonging and thriving LGBTQ+ community in Leipzig.

Our Mission

To provide Leipzig with a sober, intersectional, intergenerational, community centre where all LGBTQ+ people feel welcome and supported, can build connections, and flourish.
